About Rynos

Rynos is a small company with a attention to detail. Rynos believes the most important things are responsibility, dedication to deliver a high quality product, and customer satisfaction. After two decades, Rynos knows that no matter how small the company may be, what matters most is responsibility and professionalism. The benefit of being a smaller company is that it allows Rynos to establish a closer connection with you, our customer, which provides clearer communication in order for us to deliver the project of your dreams. We are people helping other people.

Where Rynos Began

The owner of Rynos, Walter Lopez, began with a push mower, a weed wacker, a leaf blower, and an insatiable appetite to learn more of landscaping and expand his abilities. Through hard work, long hours, and unforgiving weather conditions, Walter always delivered a flawless service to his customers. With a desire to do more, he started his own business, now known as Rynos Landscaping and Masonry LLC. Only able to provide basic lawn care and cleanup, now Rynos has expanded its capabilities, and provides an array of services including masonry, wall construction, and paving. However, while it has evolved as a business, Rynos has not forgotten its humble beginnings that have brought it to where it is today. It's goal of providing excellent attention to detail and customer satisfaction has not changed.

Services

Spring/Fall Cleanup

Pruning and trimming off dead or dry branches, raking and blowing away dead leaves in the fall, and edging flower beds. General spring cleanup.

Seeding

Removal of old, dead grass. Applying new topsoil, starter fertilizer, grass seed, and hay.

Planting

Removal of old plants, if necessary, designing the area with newer, younger plants. They're spaced out in order to give them room to grow and have a complete look once matured.

Mulching

Garden bed cleanup, including weeding, and edging. New mulch is applied tailored to the customer's specifications.

Tree Removal

Removal of dead or damaged trees, and of other trees on the property that the customer would like gone.

Masonry Steps

Removal of old steps, and replacement with new steps constructed with cinder block frame, limestone or bluestone treads, and brick or stone face depending on the customers' preference.

Retaining Walls

Digging the footing base, installing three quarter quarry process stone, and stacked blocks. All class of retaining walls including but not limited to, stacking blocks, cinder blocks, and pressure treated wood.

Pavers

Patio, sidewalk, and driveway construction. Digging footing base from 8"-15" and applying three quarter quarry process stone, stone dust, pavers, then tempering and polymer sand application for sealing. Color can vary depending on customer preference.

Sod Installation

if necessary, old grass removal or levelling terrain. Applying 2" of topsoil, insecticide applied to dirt, and starter fertilizer, all before laying down the new sod.
